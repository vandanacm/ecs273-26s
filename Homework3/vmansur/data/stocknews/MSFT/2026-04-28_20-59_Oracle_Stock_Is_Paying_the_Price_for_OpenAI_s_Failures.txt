Content
Oracle stock closed down Tuesday as its relationship with ChatGPT-developer OpenAI is under scrutiny again—and taking down much of the AI trade with it even though the tech start-up has pushed back. Its shares have fallen 15% in 2026 over concerns about its capital expenditures as it builds out artificial-intelligence data centers. The trigger for Tuesday’s slide was a report in the The Wall Street Journal that OpenAI has recently missed its own targets for new users and revenue, leading to concerns among executives about whether it will be able to fulfill its spending commitments.
