BP stock rose Tuesday after the rally in crude prices helped the British oil major to double its first-quarter profit. BP’s American depositary receipts climbed 1% to $46.40. The results cover a period where the Iran war disrupted shipping through the Strait of Hormuz, sparking a surge in oil prices.
