Title
The DHS Shutdown Hits Day 73. Nightmare Airport Lines Could Return Soon.

Date
2026-04-28 18:01

URL

Content
Travelers could again face longer lines at airport security checkpoints next month if Congress doesn’t figure out a way to fund the Department of Homeland Security this week. The federal government is on track to run out of emergency funds used to pay some DHS workers during the past month of the department’s 73-day shutdown, including Transportation Security Administration employees that staff airport screening lines. President Donald Trump had allocated $10 billion in emergency funds for DHS employees in executive orders in March and April.
